TESLA MODEL 3 WRAPPED IN RUSSET RED

TESLA MODEL 3 RUSSET RED

This is a simple, yet quite an impactful project that gives the Tesla Model 3 quite an impressive exterior look thanks to the gorgeous Russet Red wrap

Some of our customers like to keep things simple. Beyond the wrap, some of them don’t really want to go. And there’s nothing wrong with that. For this particular 2021 Tesla Model 3 Dual Motor, we gave it a completely brand new look, thanks to a full vehicle color-change vinyl wrap in KPMF Matte Russet Red. The end result is one of the most appealing exterior finishes for a Tesla we’ve yet seen, and the owner is quite happy too.

This copper shade of red looks spectacular on the Model 3, but it’s still more understated than some of the brighter red finishes. And for most owners, that’s the look they are going after. In turn, thanks to the wide variety of KPMF wraps we’re able to offer, our customers can turn their vehicle into any shade of red or pretty much any color out there that they might like.

If you are interested in our a complete concourse detailing service for your Tesla Model 3, please don’t hesitate to contact us right away. Our sales team will provide you with a detailed quote for the services required right away.

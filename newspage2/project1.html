2022 BLACK SAPPHIRE METALLIC BMW IX XDRIVE50 – MULTI-STAGE PAINT CORRECTION, XPEL PPF, CERAMIC COATING

2022 BLACK SAPPHIRE METALLIC BMW IX XDRIVE50

The owner of this 2022 Black Sapphire Metallic BMW iX xDrive50 came to our Los Angeles location seeking a multi-stage paint correction, XPEL PPF (Paint Protection Film), window tint, and ceramic coating, and the result of our work is one gorgeous-looking electric SAV that will turn heads anywhere it goes

The 2022 BMW iX xDrive50 is an $84,100 (starting price) all-electric SAV (Sports Activity Vehicle) from the German car maker that boasts an estimated range of up to 324 miles (521 kilometers). It’s a rather large, spacious, and luxurious vehicle that is sized similarly to the highly-coveted BMW X5 – on the outside. Interior-wise, the electric SAV gives more space in the cabin and the luxury element is heightened to a new level, even when compared to the BMW X5. The owner of this particular Black Sapphire Metallic BMW iX xDrive50 came over to us for a paint correction, debadging, PPF (Paint Protection Film), window tinting, and ceramic coating.

The vehicle received a multi-stage paint correction, ensuring this pristine and brand new vehicle and its paint is leveled, cleaned, and correct so it can be protected for years to come. Furthermore, we’ve added a complete front-end XPEL Ultimate Plus paint protection film alongside the lower body high-impact areas, ensuring the utmost protection to the vehicle without adding the paint protection film on the whole vehicle. Additionally, the badges on the vehicle were removed, cleaning up the exterior and pushing the blacked-out look to a new level.

The owner wanted to tint the windows, matching the entire car with a similar window tint, done with an XPEL Prime XR premium ceramic window tint. The wheels were also taken care of, receiving a full wheel barrel and caliper ceramic coating, ensuring easier brake dust cleanup, protection, and improved looks for years to come.

Finally, our team applied a complete vehicle ceramic coating. In this case, we went with a full vehicle Gyeon Q2 Infinite Base Type 1 ceramic coating, ensuring both pristine looks and added protection to the paint finish of this vehicle. The resulting look speaks for itself, as the Black Sapphire Metallic paint finish on this BMW iX looks incredible from all angles, as you can see for yourself in the media gallery right below.

If you own a BMW iX and want to protect your investment, please don’t hesitate to contact us right away. Our team will gladly help you get the same result you want for your vehicle.

About Us

At Detaild, we are Las Vegas's automotive excellence hub, specializing in XPEL film, 3M tint, Ceramic Pro, and Gyeon Quartz coatings. Our commitment to perfection is evident in our color-change programs, concours detailing, and certified ceramic coating services. Tailoring protection packages to unique preferences, we redefine aesthetics with vinyl wrappings and ensure long-lasting quality with a 10-year warranty on paint protection films. Join us on your automotive journey for unmatched quality and genuine value.

As our company expands, our commitment remains unchanged.

At Detaild, our dedication to exceptional customer service sets us apart. We recognize that your vehicle is not just a means of transportation; it's an expression of your identity. With the same care and respect we extend to our own vehicles, we ensure your car receives unparalleled attention. Whether you seek to maintain the pristine appearance of your everyday vehicle or wish to revive a classic car to its former splendor, our team of specialists is at your service.

Detaild stands out for its meticulous attention to every nuance and unwavering commitment to excellence. When you choose us, you entrust your vehicle to the finest automotive detailing services in the industry. Contact us today to schedule your appointment and discover firsthand the distinctive "Detaild" experience. Your satisfaction is our priority.

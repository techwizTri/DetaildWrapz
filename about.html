About Us

At Detaild, we are Las Vegas's automotive excellence hub, specializing in XPEL film, 3M tint, Ceramic Pro, and Gyeon Quartz coatings. Our commitment to perfection is evident in our color-change programs, concours detailing, and certified ceramic coating services. Tailoring protection packages to unique preferences, we redefine aesthetics with vinyl wrappings and ensure long-lasting quality with a 10-year warranty on paint protection films. Join us on your automotive journey for unmatched quality and genuine value.

As our company grows, the focus stays the same.

We are committed to providing an unparalleled level of customer service. We understand that your car is more than just a mode of transportation, it's an extension of your personality, and we treat it with the same care and respect that we would our own. We strive to make the detailing process as convenient as possible by bringing the detail to you. Whether you're looking to keep your daily driver looking its best, or want to restore a classic car to its former glory, our team of experts is here to help. With our attention to detail and commitment to excellence, you can trust us to provide the best automotive detailing services in the industry. Contact us today to schedule your appointment and experience the "Detaild" difference.
